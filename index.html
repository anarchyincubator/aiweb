Be the Hero we Need

It's time to directly build apps to directly help people with their most important needs - like food, housing, and healthcare. Instead of letting profits drive our app infrastructure, lets focus on, from the bottom-up, empowering communities with the tools they need to thrive together.

Our Approach

We are building open source, community driven, location based apps to connect communities with the goods and services they need. For example, Eggslist is our already-built platform to connect local food producers (like gardeners, homesteaders, and farmers) directly to people in their community.

Our platforms function like a user-experience friendly Craigslist - enter your zip and search categories and subcategories of items or services near you, with easily editable categories to meet needs.

Our platforms are free, able to be self-hosted and deployed anywhere, and easily modified for different uses. Deploy your own community food network, a support network to help people meet each other's needs during natural disasters, or create a digital mutual aid network in your city.

Projects We Are Building

Eggslist

Our already-deployed platform for building local, sustainable food communities. For consumers, search by location for food products near you. For food producers, post and manage listings, create a bio page about you, and create blog posts about what you do.

Local Tradesmen

Build our platform into a local directory of tradespeople - all for free. Support local workers and businesses without having to pay monthly fees, or as a tradesperson, lose income to a corporation that belongs to you - the people doing the work.

Disaster Relief

Easily deploy platform to help with human-to-human disaster relief, posting specific needs or help and services that you have available. Pull together to suppor the families and people in your community to pull through together!
