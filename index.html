Our Approach

Self-Hosted. Community-Owned. Forkable.

Every tool we build is a seed you can plant in your own soil. Deploy it on your own server, modify it for your community's needs, and share it forward.

  • Deployable

    Spin up your own instance with a single command. No cloud lock-in, no subscription fees.

  • Forkable

    Every repo is designed to be forked and adapted. Build a food network, a tool library, or something we haven't imagined.

  • Community-First

    Decisions are made in the open. Contributors shape the roadmap. No investors, no board — just people building together.
