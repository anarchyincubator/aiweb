Eggslist

Food freedom! Eggslist is a community network for local food. Keep backyard chickens and have extra eggs? Grow produce in your garden and have extra to share with your neighbor? Eggslist is the platform for you!

Built to be easily deployable and customizable by local groups with specific needs.

Hatchtrack

Hatchtrack is a community of poultry enthusiasts. It's designed to monitor and track egg incubation characteristics to ensure the best hatches.

The data we collect will allow for better hatching and conservation efforts.
